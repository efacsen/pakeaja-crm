Implementation Strategy

Phase 1 Focus (Next 3 Months): Build the revenue engine. Without contacts, calculator, pipeline, and quotes, we have no business. These features directly enable sales and must work flawlessly on mobile.

Phase 2 Focus (Months 4-6): Operational excellence. Project management and field operations ensure we can deliver what we sell. Quality control builds reputation for premium pricing.

Phase 3 Focus (Months 7-9): Scale and optimize. Client portal reduces support load. Analytics reveal optimization opportunities. Inventory management improves margins.

Future Vision: Innovation and differentiation. AI, IoT, and advanced features position us as industry leaders but only after nailing the fundamentals.

Key Success Factor: Mobile-first development with offline capability. 60% of users will be in the field on phones/tablets with intermittent connectivity. Every feature must work seamlessly on mobile or it's not worth building.
