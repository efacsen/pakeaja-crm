**Welcome to Horizon Suite!** This guide will get you up and running in just 5 minutes.

## 🎯 What You'll Learn

In this quick start guide, you'll learn how to:

* 🔐 Log in to your account
* 📊 Navigate the dashboard
* 🏃‍♂️ Create your first canvassing report
* 🔥 Manage deals in the sales pipeline
* 📱 Use mobile features for field work

## 📋 Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

* ✅ **Account Access**: Your login credentials from your administrator
* ✅ **Device**: Computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet connection
* ✅ **Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest version)
* ✅ **Permissions**: Appropriate role (Sales Rep, Sales Manager, or Admin)

## 🚀 Step 1: Login & First Look

### 🔐 Logging In

1. **Open your browser** and navigate to your Horizon Suite URL
2. **Enter your credentials**:
   * Email address
   * Password
3. **Select your role** (if prompted):
   * 👤 **Sales Rep**: Field sales activities
   * 👥 **Sales Manager**: Team oversight and analytics
   * ⚙️ **Admin**: System configuration

## 🏃‍♂️ Step 2: Create Your First Canvassing Report

### 📱 Mobile-First Approach

Canvassing is designed for **field work**, so it works perfectly on mobile devices:

1. **Navigate to Canvassing**
   * Click **"Canvassing"** in the main menu
   * Or use the mobile app

2. **Create New Report**
   * Click **"Laporan Baru"** (New Report)
   * Fill in the required information:

#### 🏢 Company Information
```
Company Name: PT. Contoh Industri
Contact Person: Budi Santoso
Contact Position: Manager Purchasing
Phone: 0812-3456-7890
Email: budi@contoh.co.id
```

#### 🎯 Visit Details
```
Visit Date: Today's date
Visit Outcome: Tertarik (Interested)
Project Segment: Protective Coatings
```

#### 💰 Project Potential
```
Potential Type: Value
Estimated Value: Rp 500,000,000
```

#### 📅 Follow-up Planning
```
Next Action: Send Proposal
Next Action Date: 3 days from today
Notes: Interested in warehouse floor coating
```

3. **Add Documentation**
   * 📷 Take photos of current coating conditions
   * 📍 Get GPS location (automatic)
   * 📝 Add general notes

4. **Submit Report**
   * Click **"Kirim Laporan"** (Send Report)
   * Report will sync automatically

{% hint style="info" %}
**Offline Mode**: If you're offline, the report will be saved locally and sync when you're back online!
{% endhint %}

## 🔥 Step 3: Manage Your Sales Pipeline

### 🌡️ Understanding the Temperature System

Horizon Suite uses a unique **temperature system** to prioritize deals:

| Temperature | Range | Status | Action Needed |
|-------------|-------|--------|---------------|
| 🧊 **Cold** | 0-25°C | New leads | Schedule contact |
| 🔥 **Warm** | 26-50°C | Engaged prospects | Regular follow-up |
| 🌋 **Hot** | 51-75°C | Active negotiation | Focus attention |
| ⚡ **Critical** | 76-100°C | Closing soon | Daily contact |

### 📊 Pipeline Navigation

1. **Go to Sales Pipeline**
   * Click **"Sales"** → **"Pipeline"**

2. **View Your Deals**
   * See deals organized by stage
   * Use **drag & drop** to move deals
   * Click on cards for details

3. **Use Quick Actions**
   * 📞 **Call**: Log phone calls
   * 📧 **Email**: Send follow-ups
   * 📄 **Quote**: Create proposals
   * 🚗 **Visit**: Schedule site visits

## 📱 Step 4: Mobile Features

### 🔄 Offline Capabilities

Horizon Suite works **offline** for field sales:

* ✅ **Create canvassing reports** without internet
* ✅ **View existing deals** and customer information
* ✅ **Log activities** and update deal status
* ✅ **Take photos** with GPS coordinates
* ✅ **Auto-sync** when back online

### 📍 GPS Integration

* **Automatic location tracking** for canvassing reports
* **Route optimization** for field visits
* **Territory mapping** for sales coverage

### 📷 Photo Documentation

* **Visual proof** of opportunities
* **Before/after** coating conditions
* **Automatic GPS tagging** for location reference

## 🎯 Step 5: Daily Workflow

### 🌅 Morning Routine

1. **Check Dashboard**
   * Review hot deals (>50°C)
   * Check today's scheduled activities
   * Review overnight notifications

2. **Plan Your Day**
   * Prioritize critical deals
   * Schedule canvassing visits
   * Prepare follow-up materials

### 🏃‍♂️ Field Work

1. **Canvassing**
   * Use mobile app for reports
   * Take photos and GPS coordinates
   * Set immediate follow-up actions

2. **Customer Visits**
   * Update deal status in real-time
   * Log activities and outcomes
   * Schedule next actions

### 🌆 End of Day

1. **Sync Data**
   * Ensure all offline data is synced
   * Review completed activities
   * Update deal temperatures

2. **Plan Tomorrow**
   * Review pipeline health
   * Schedule follow-up activities
   * Prepare for next day's visits
